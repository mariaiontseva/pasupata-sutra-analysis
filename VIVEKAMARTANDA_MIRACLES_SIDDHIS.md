## CATEGORY 4: THE EIGHT SUPERNATURAL POWERS (AṢṬA-SIDDHI)

### 11. **The Eight Powers** (V. 145, 146, 174)
Explicitly mentioned as attainable through:
- Khecarī mudrā and nectar drinking (V. 145)
- Body filled with nectar of immortality (V. 146)
- Meditation on chakra locations (V. 174)

**Specific power mentioned:**
- **V. 146:** "powers of becoming as small as an atom" (aṇimā siddhi)

*Note: The traditional eight siddhis are:*
1. *Aṇimā* - becoming as small as an atom ✓ (explicitly mentioned)
2. *Mahimā* - becoming infinitely large
3. *Laghimā* - becoming weightless/levitation
4. *Garimā* - becoming infinitely heavy
5. *Prāpti* - ability to reach anywhere
6. *Prākāmya* - obtaining wishes
7. *Īśitva* - lordship over creation
8. *Vaśitva* - control over all beings

### 12. **Ability to Attract Siddha Women**
